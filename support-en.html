Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is my data stored?
    Calculation history and presets are stored only on your device and are never sent to external servers.
  • What happens to my data if I delete the app?
    All saved data will be deleted when you uninstall the app.

When Contacting Us

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include the following information in your inquiry:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Pro)
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • Detailed description of the issue
