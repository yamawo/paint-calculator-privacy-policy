Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This app "Paint Calculator" (hereinafter "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information. All data such as calculation history and presets are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Storage

Data created in the App (calculation history, preset settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device. When you delete the App, this data will also be deleted.

4. Contact

For inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store app page.
